Tween Night at the Library

If you're between 10 and 14 years old get ready to take over the library this Friday, November 4th! Come just us for a fun-filled night of games, activities, and food. 7pm-9pm in the Teen Room!

Calling All Craft Lovers!

Join us Tuesday, November 15th for our annual Hunting for Crafts day! We've got a lot of fun activties planned that are sure to get your creative juices flowing! Check out the calendar for more information.

Do you want to learn English or improve your English skills?

Sign up for the English Conversation class on the Friends of the Library page or at the main desk in the library. We meet every Wednesday at 6pm, come as often as you can! We look forward to seeing you there.

And we want YOU to check them out! Choose either the Teen Select or Best Sellers loaded Kindle and start reading! Head over to the Kindle Page to find out what titles we currently offer.
